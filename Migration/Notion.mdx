title: "Notion"
---

Mysamantha lets you move your existing notes from Notion into [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai) without manual copying.

\
Your content is brought in as notes so you can continue working without starting over.

## **What Gets Imported**

When you import from Notion:

- Text content is preserved
- Headings and structure are maintained
- Notes are created inside **Notes**

## **How to Import from Notion**

**Steps**

1. Open **Notes** in [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai)
2. Click the **Kebab menu**
3. Select **Import from Notion**
4. Sign in to your Notion account
5. Choose the pages or workspace to import

The import starts automatically.

## **After Import**

Once imported:

- Each Notion page becomes a note
- Titles are generated from page names
- You can edit, label, pin, or organize notes normally

Imported notes behave the same as any other note.

## **Using Imported Notes with Samantha**

After migration, you can ask Samantha to:

- Summarize imported notes
- Turn notes into to dos
- Explain or rewrite content
- Find information from old notes

This helps make existing content actionable.

## **Offline Behavior**

- Import requires an internet connection
- Imported notes are available offline once synced

## **Privacy & Control**

- Data is encrypted
- Imported content is not sold or used to train AI models
- You can delete imported notes anytime
